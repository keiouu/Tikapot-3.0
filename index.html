    Tikapot-3.0

    Tikapot-3.0 is a streaming templating engine written in node.js

    The aim is to develop a system that serves large, content-rich sites that can be easily edited in-browser by 1000's of collaborators. To this end there will be advanced caching and optimization techniques incorporated into the system as well as a powerful in-browser editor. There must also be a fine-grained permissions system.
